About Barokah

Founded in 2010, Barokah Travel has been facilitating spiritual journeys with sincerity and excellence. Our name, meaning "blessing," reflects our commitment to making your pilgrimage a truly blessed experience.

With over a decade of experience, our team of dedicated professionals understands the unique needs of pilgrims. We combine traditional values with modern travel expertise to create journeys that nourish both body and soul.
